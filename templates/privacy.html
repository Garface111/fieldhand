FIELDHAND is an AI-powered SMS business assistant for independent trade contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Who We Are

FIELDHAND is operated by Dyson Swarm Technologies. We provide an AI-powered business management tool delivered over SMS, helping independent trade contractors manage jobs, invoices, expenses, and client communications.

Contact: support@fieldhand.app
Website: fieldhand-api-production.up.railway.app

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up or use FIELDHAND, we collect the following:

  • Contact Information: Your name, mobile phone number, and email address (if provided)
  • Business Information: Job details, client names and contact info, expense records, and invoice data you share with us via SMS
  • Usage Data: SMS message history, timestamps, and service interactions
  • Opt-In Records: IP address, browser/device information, and timestamp captured at the moment of SMS consent for compliance purposes
  • Documents: Quotes, invoices, and other documents generated on your behalf

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide the FIELDHAND service:

  • Sending and receiving SMS messages as part of your business assistant service
  • Storing and organizing your business data (jobs, clients, invoices, expenses)
  • Generating professional documents (quotes, invoices, change orders) using your business data
  • Sending proactive business alerts, reminders, and summaries
  • Processing payments via Stripe (we never store card data)
  • Maintaining consent records required by telecom regulations

4. SMS Messaging

When you provide your phone number and consent to receive messages, you agree to receive SMS communications from FIELDHAND related to your business activity. Message frequency varies based on your usage — typically 2–10 messages per day when actively using the service.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
For help: Reply HELP to any message or email support@fieldhand.app.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

5. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information or phone number to any third party
  • We do not share your data with marketing companies or advertisers
  • We do not send unsolicited promotional or marketing SMS messages
  • We do not use your data or SMS conversations to train AI models
  • We do not store payment card information (handled entirely by Stripe)

6. Third-Party Services

FIELDHAND uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery and phone number management (Twilio Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic — AI processing of your messages (no data retention by Anthropic for API users)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Railway — Cloud hosting infrastructure

Each third party is bound by their own privacy policies. We do not authorize them to use your data for purposes beyond providing their services to us.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in encrypted databases hosted on Railway's secure cloud infrastructure in the United States. SMS communications are transmitted via Twilio's encrypted infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion of all your data at any time (see Section 9).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Portability — request an export of your business data

9. Data Deletion

To request deletion of all your data, text DELETE to your FIELDHAND number or email support@fieldhand.app with the subject "Delete My Data." We will permanently delete all your records within 30 days and confirm via email.

10. Children's Privacy

FIELDHAND is a business tool intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active users of material changes via SMS. Continued use of FIELDHAND after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The current version is always available at this URL.

Questions or Concerns?

Email us at support@fieldhand.app or reply HELP to any FIELDHAND message. We respond within 1 business day.
