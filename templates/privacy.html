Privacy Policy & SMS Terms

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

FIELDHAND is an AI-powered business assistant for independent trade contractors. We provide SMS-based tools for job tracking, invoicing, expense logging, and client communication.

SMS Terms of Service

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive text messages from FIELDHAND related to your business, including:

  • Job status updates and reminders
  • Invoice notifications and payment confirmations
  • Expense logging confirmations
  • Budget alerts and business reminders
  • Responses to messages you send us

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your business activity. Typically 2–10 messages per day when actively using the service.

Message & Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out: Reply STOP to any message at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

How to Get Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or email support@fieldhand.app.

Supported Carriers: All major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and regional carriers.

Information We Collect

When you sign up or use FIELDHAND, we collect:

  • Your name and mobile phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Business information you share (jobs, clients, expenses)
  • SMS conversation history
  • IP address and browser info at time of opt-in (for consent records)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the FIELDHAND service:

  • Sending and receiving SMS messages on your behalf
  • Storing your business data (jobs, clients, invoices, expenses)
  • Generating documents (quotes, invoices) using your business information
  • Providing proactive business alerts and summaries

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your information to third parties
  • We do not share your phone number with marketers
  • We do not send unsolicited promotional messages
  • We do not use your data to train AI models

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion of all your data at any time by texting DELETE to FIELDHAND or emailing support@fieldhand.app. We will delete all records within 30 days of your request.

Security

Your data is stored in encrypted databases. SMS communications are transmitted via Twilio's secure infrastructure. We never store payment card information — all payments are processed by Stripe.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@fieldhand.app or reply HELP to any FIELDHAND message.
